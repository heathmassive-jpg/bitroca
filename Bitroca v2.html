What is Bitroca

A game that behaves
like a market.

Not another sandbox. A real-time voxel economy where scarcity, speculation, and skill all matter. Here's what you're getting into.

01 / MINECORE

Mine & forge

Proof-of-work puzzles inside every block. Crack the hash, claim the reward — FORGE tokens credited to your wallet, on-chain style, in-game.

> EXPECTED YIELD · 0.4 FORGE/min
02 / OWNCORE

Personal worlds

Stake a claim, set the rules. Private build, public trade zone, pvp arena — your call. Friends list or public leaderboard, your world is yours.

> CLAIM SIZE · 128×128×256
03 / TRADECORE

The CIPHER market

Every resource has a price and every price moves. Trade FORGE, IRON, DIAM, GOLD and rare drops on the open order book. Liquidity is a real skill.

> DAILY VOL · $1.4M FORGE
04 / RUNLIVE

Always online

One persistent server. Your walls stay up when you log off. 488 days uptime, no resets, no wipes. The world ages whether you're in it or not.

> UPTIME · 99.94% YTD
05 / CLIMBMETA

Tiered progression

Wood → stone → iron → diamond → cipher. Each tier unlocks new depth, new ore, new risk. The leaderboard is on the map, not in a menu.

> TIER 5 HOLDERS · 14
06 / TALKSOCIAL

Live comms

Proximity voice, world chat, trade broadcast. When someone strikes diamond at y=−48, everyone feels it on the ticker.

> PEAK CONCURRENT · 421

Dev feed · v0.48

Shipped last week.

We ship constantly. Here's the last three patch notes, straight from the build server. The full changelog lives on the server motd.

2026.04.18 · v0.48.2PATCH

Deep-cave overhaul

Y-depth below −96 is now procedurally richer. Cipher nodes spawn with 3× density, but cave-ins are twice as likely. Bring support beams.

  • New biome: Ashglass caverns (y<−160)
  • New ore: Nullstone (tier 6)
  • Pickaxe durability rebalanced
  • Removed infinite lava dupe
2026.04.11 · v0.48.1FEATURE

Market orders, finally

Limit & stop orders on the CIPHER exchange. Liquidity providers get a 0.1% rebate on fills. Yes, you can finally set it and forget it.

  • Order book UI, live depth chart
  • FORGE/USD oracle from 3 signers
  • Wallet multi-sig for guild treasuries
2026.04.04 · v0.48.0HOTFIX

World stability pass

Fixed the chunk-load desync that was eating blocks at world edges. Also: the bug where dropping stone inside water was pricing it as diamond.

  • Chunk sync at world borders
  • Stone→diamond pricing exploit
  • Lowered server tick to 60Hz (was 40)
  • New /status command
