WHAT IS BITROCA

Everything you need
to build an empire.

One world. Real stakes. A live economy that never sleeps.

01 ⛏️

Mine & Forge

Break blocks, mine resources, and solve Proof-of-Work puzzles to earn FORGE tokens in a live blockchain-inspired economy.

02 🌍

Personal Worlds

Own and build your own persistent world. Set access rules, invite friends, or open it to everyone on the server.

03 💰

Real Economy

Trade resources and currencies with other players. Buy and sell on the CIPHER market. Every transaction is logged.

04 🌐

Always Online

A live VPS-hosted server means the world keeps going even when you log off. Your progress is always saved.

05 🏆

Level Up

Progress through pickaxe tiers, unlock new abilities, and climb the ranks as you mine deeper and trade smarter.

06 💬

Multiplayer Chat

Talk to players in your world in real time. See announcements when players forge new blocks or join for the first time.
